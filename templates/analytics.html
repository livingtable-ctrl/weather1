Brier Score
—
lower = better · 0.25 = random · 0.0 = perfect

The Brier Score measures forecast accuracy: for each settled prediction we square the error between our probability and the actual outcome (0 or 1), then average them. A score of 0.0 means every prediction was perfectly confident and correct. A score of 0.25 is what you'd get from always guessing 50/50 — the baseline to beat.
